# Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation

Goal: lock the business model and create the first production-ready delivery base.

### Step 1

Choose the first niche.

Locked first niche:

1. home services

Next niche after repeatability:

1. fitness studios / gyms

### Step 2

Define the service structure.

Deliverables:

- Launch / Growth / Premium tier definitions
- scope boundaries
- recurring offers
- rough pricing logic

### Step 3

Design the starter architecture.

Deliverables:

- Next.js base app
- Payload CMS structure
- design token system
- reusable block library
- template-family plan

## Phase 2: Sales and Delivery Workflow

Goal: make the business operable before over-automating it.

### Step 4

Create the shared business schema.

Deliverables:

- Lead
- Opportunity
- Client Brief
- Proposal
- Project
- Support Account

### Step 5

Build the minimum agent workflows.

Priority order:

1. lead research + qualification
2. outreach drafting
3. meeting summary + discovery extraction
4. proposal drafting
5. project bootstrap
6. QA support

### Step 6

Build the client-facing starting assets.

Deliverables:

- lean agency website
- one to three niche demos
- call booking flow
- simple sales collateral

## Phase 3: Optimization

Goal: improve what works instead of guessing.

### Step 7

Start real outreach and client conversations.

Measure:

- reply rate
- meeting rate
- close rate
- delivery speed
- revision count

### Step 8

Add optimization loops.

First refinement targets:

1. outreach
2. discovery summaries
3. proposal generation
4. qualification scoring
5. QA signals

## Time Framing

### 30 days

Target: MVP that supports real work.

### 60 to 90 days

Target: stable system with better reliability and clearer metrics.

### 3 to 6 months

Target: mature, refined, agency-grade operating rhythm.
