# Marketing And Social

This file defines how marketing and social-media work should fit into the agency operating system.

## Short Answer

Yes, you will probably want a marketing or social-content agent eventually.

No, it should not be an early priority compared with:

1. lead research
2. outreach
3. discovery
4. proposals
5. delivery quality

## Why It Is Lower Priority

In the beginning, your biggest growth risk is not lack of social posting. It is lack of a validated niche offer and a reliable delivery system.

Social media becomes more useful after:

1. the agency has a clear niche story
2. there are demo templates or case-study assets to show
3. the brand positioning is clear

## Recommended Marketing Scope For V1

Keep V1 marketing simple:

1. agency site
2. clear service pages
3. niche demo templates
4. a few before-and-after style teardown assets
5. outreach-driven sales motion

## When To Add A Marketing Agent

Add one after the first real offer and template family are credible.

Good responsibilities:

1. repurpose teardown insights into posts
2. create educational local-business content
3. draft carousel or short-form social content
4. support case-study storytelling

Do not expect a marketing agent to replace the core sales system.

## Suggested Future Agent

### Marketing Content Agent

Possible responsibilities:

1. draft LinkedIn and X posts
2. draft carousel content
3. repurpose audits into educational content
4. maintain a lightweight editorial backlog

Human review should remain required before publishing.

## Guardrails

1. do not publish generic AI content just to stay active
2. do not let social content distract from outbound and delivery
3. tie content to the chosen niche and actual offer
4. require human review for public publishing early on

## Definition Of Done For Marketing V1

Marketing is good enough for V1 when:

1. the agency site explains the offer clearly
2. there are credible demo assets
3. outbound can reference those assets
4. at least a small content backlog exists if social is used
